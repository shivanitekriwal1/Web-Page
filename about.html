About us

Technology means a skillful system. The etymology of technology illuminates its meaning. Techne in Greek means art, craft or skill. From this, Ancient Greeks derived the word technologia, which means a system. This word is made from techne plus logia. Logia comes from logos with means both word and knowledge.From this, we get the English word technology.
